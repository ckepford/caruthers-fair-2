Fresno Motorcycle Club

35th Annual Races being held at Caruthers Fair Short Track

The 35th annual amateur T.T. motorcycle and quad races and Go Carts are coming to the Caruthers District Fair, Thursday, Friday and Saturday, during the Fair.
The Fresno Motorcycle Club, which has 30 members and was chartered in 1940. The club is dedicated to family values and family participation in their many charity events.
In the past 35 years, Caruthers has gone from an unknown town on the racing circuit, to the most sought after three-day event in the state. This fact becomes evident as you see the riders coming in from Los Angeles to Sacramento and Gilroy to Oakhurst. The most cited reason by the riders are track condition and organization.
